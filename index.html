There are millions of dogs that enter dog shelters and only half ever find their forever-family. At Adopt-A-Pet, we want our loveable dogs to find the homes they deserve. From puppies to seniors, there is a dog that would be perfect for your home!

We want every dog to have a home. That's why we have partnered with numerous shelters across the country to reach as many families as possible. You can view the dogs available in a shelter near you, along with their preferences and stories.
